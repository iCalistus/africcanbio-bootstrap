African Biosiences

African Biosciences Ltd. is a pioneer and innovative life sciences company providing research reagents, supplies and technical support services to the academic, research, healthcare diagnostics and forensic testing sectors in Nigeria and across Africa. We are dedicated to transforming life sciences research by providing scientific and technical support to the academic and research community by enabling and catalyzing the ability to conduct bioscience research.

State of The Art Biotech Lab

Biotech Research Lab

Our state of the art Biotech Research Lab, provides facilities to carry out genomics based research. From DNA extraction to Real Time PCR. We also carry out contract research services where our well qualified technicians undertake certain aspects of the lab work and provide results to customer for interpretation and use. Our Lab services are denominated in such a way that you could select to only certain aspects of your lab work with us and take care of the others by yourself.
